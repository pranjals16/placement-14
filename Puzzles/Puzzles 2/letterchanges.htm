Word & Letter Play
 
Letter Changes 		 
How many other three-letter words can you make from the word say by changing only one letter and not moving around the remaining two? Set yourself a goal of 26. Each of the 26 must appear as an entry word in a college-level dictionary. No proper nouns allowed.

Ready for another challenge? How many other five-letter words can you make from the word share by changing only one letter at a time and not moving around the remaining four? This time your goal should be 15. Each of the 15 must appear as an entry word in a college-level dictionary. No proper nouns allowed.


 
say
bay, cay, day, fay, gay, hay, jay, lay, may, nay, pay, ray, way
shy, sky, sly, spy, sty, soy
sac, sad, sag, sap, sat, saw, sax

share
shard, shark, sharp
shade, shake, shale, shame, shape, shave
shire, shore, spare, scare, snare, stare

Based on a puzzle in Ross Eckler, Making the Alphabet Dance: Recreational Wordplay. New York, St. Martin's Press, 1996.
