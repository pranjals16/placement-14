Word & Letter Play
 
One More 		 
Figure out what the capitalized words in Column A have in common. Then decide which one of the three lowercased words in Column B belongs with the capitalized ones.

Column A
DESSERTS
DIAPER
STRAP
REWARD
STAR
STINK		 Column B
tower
devil
exits


 
The word from Column B that belongs with the words in Column A is devil. Like the six words in Column A, devil spelled backward makes another English word.

STRATEGY: Look for a pattern.

Source: item #6 on p. 108 in Big Book of Games II, New York: Workman, 1988.
