Insects and Spiders		  
As you know, one way to tell an insect from a spider is to count its legs. All insects have six legs, and all spiders have eight legs. So if some insects and spiders went to a dance, and there were 48 dancing legs, how many insects and how many spiders were at the dance?


 
Four insects (24 legs) and three spiders (24 legs). No other combination will work.

Lowell House, Brain Games, p.48
