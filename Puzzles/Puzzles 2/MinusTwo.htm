Number and Math Play

Minus Two		  
How many times can you subtract the number 2 from the number 32?
  		 
Once. After you subtract 2 from 32, you subtract 2 from 30, from 28, and so on.
 
Source: Inspired by a puzzle in Mensa Presents Mighty Brain Teasers, R. Allen & J. Fulton. New York: Barnes and Noble, 1999.
