Proverb Square 		 
The letters in the grid below spell out a common proverb. Start with one of the letters in the top row, and continue moving to the left or right, up or down, to find the hidden sentence. You may not move diagonally.

N E A N N
R D P E Y
A E P A S
E N A V E
Y N S I D


 
If you start with the A in the top row, move down for P, to the right for E, and up for N, you will be on your way to finding the proverb "A penny saved is a penny earned."

Based on a puzzle in The World's Most Amazing Puzzles by Charles Barry Townsend. New York: Sterling Publishing, 1993.
