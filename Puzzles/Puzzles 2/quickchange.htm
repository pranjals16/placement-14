Word & Letter Play
 
Quick Change 		 
What seven-letter, one-syllable, past-tense verb becomes a seven-letter, three-syllable, past-tense verb if you change its fifth letter from an H to a T?


 
The word reached becomes reacted.

From Raymond D. Love, "Word to the Wise" in "Wild Cards," edited by Paddy Smith. Games magazine, June 2000.
