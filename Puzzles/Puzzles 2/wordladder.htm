Word Ladder 		 
The creator of Alice's Adventures in Wonderland, Lewis Carroll, was a word lover. Here's a kind of game he played. It's called a word ladder.

Remove one letter from word #1, and replace it with another letter at the same place to make word #2; then remove a letter from word #2 and replace it with another letter at the same place to make word #3; and so on until you end up, at word #6, with an antonym for word #1.

    L  E  S  S
  1. _  _  _  _
  2. _  _  _  _
  3. _  _  _  _
  4. _  _  _  _
  5. _  _  _  _


 
  1. L E S S
  2. L O S S
  3. L O S E
  4. L O V E
  5. M O V E
  6. M O R E

Based on a puzzle in Ross Eckler, Making the Alphabet Dance: Recreational Wordplay. New York, St. Martin's Press, 1996.
