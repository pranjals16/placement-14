The Property Game 		 
What property do all the words in Group 1 and none of the words in Group 2 have? hint: Think of possible suffixes.

Group 1: advance, base, battle, fig, pig
Group 2: proceed, top, fight, fruit, animal


 
All the words in Group 1 can take the suffix -ment; none of the words in Group 2 can.
