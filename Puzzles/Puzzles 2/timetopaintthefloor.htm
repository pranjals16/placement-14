Time to Paint the Floor		  
If it takes Polly the Painter 1 hour to paint a bedroom floor that is 9 feet wide and 12 feet long, how long will it take her to paint the living room floor, which is twice as wide and twice as long?


  
Four hours. The size of a room that measures 9’ x 12’ is 108 square feet. A room that is twice as long and twice as wide will measure 18’ by 24’, which makes 432 square feet. That makes the living room 4 times the size of the bedroom, so it will take 4 times as long to paint.
