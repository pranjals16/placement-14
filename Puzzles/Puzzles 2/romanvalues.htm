Roman Values		  
What is the second to the largest number and the second to the smallest number that you can make if you have one each of the following Roman numerals?

I V L X


 
The second to the largest number is LXIV (64); the second to the smallest number is XLVI (46).

STRATEGY: Make a list.
I = 1
V = 5
L = 50
X = 10
The only combinations are: 44, 46, 64, 66

Inspired by a puzzle in Mighty Mini Mind Bogglers, K. C. Richards, New York: Sterling, 1999.
