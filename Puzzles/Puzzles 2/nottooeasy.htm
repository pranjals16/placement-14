Not Too Easy 		 
English words containing the letter combinations ii or uu or aa or ww or vv are rare. Can you think of one of each? Don't suggest aardvark as the example for aa; it's too easy.


 
skiing; taxiing; radii
vacuum; continuum
bazaar
powwow
savvy; flivver

From More Puzzles for Pleasure and Leisure by Thomas L. Hirsch. New York: Abelard-Schuman, 1974.
