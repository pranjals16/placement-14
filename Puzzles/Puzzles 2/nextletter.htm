Next Letter, Please 		 
What is the next letter in the following series?

A C F H K M ___


 
The next letter is P. The given letters progress as follows:

Skip 1 letter, skip 2 letters, skip 1 letter, skip 2 letters, skip 1 letter. So the next letter is 2 letters forward, or P.

Based on a puzzle in Terry Stickels, "Mind Boggling." American Way, May 1, 2000.
